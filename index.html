Summer Camp Information

Do your kids get bored at home? We came up with a summer camp idea to end this boredom with fun and creativity. From June 15 - 17, we will be hosting a summer camp for kids ages 4-14. This event will happen at the Sonterra big park 9am - 12pm. In the big park, we will set up a canopy like shade for kids to be cool in. Kids will engage in creative activies such as, origami, arts&crafts, and many enhancing competitions, kids will also participate in physical activities like sports and games. Due to COVID19 slowly decreasing, wearing masks is optional. We will provide drinks and snacks, but sugest that you kids bring their own water because of the Arizona heat. Price will be $20 for 3 days. Hosted by Tushar, Ishaan, and Anshul.
